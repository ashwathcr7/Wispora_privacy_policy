## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Wispora** handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience. Wispora does **not collect, store, or share any personal information** from users.

---

## 2. Device Permissions & Camera Usage

Wispora requires access to your device's camera to deliver its core functionality:

- **Camera Access:** The camera is used solely to capture images of clouds for classification.
- **Ephemeral Processing (No Storage):** The captured image is temporarily processed to predict the cloud type. **We do not save, store, or retain your photos.** The photo is immediately and permanently deleted from memory as soon as the classification result is shown to you. It is never saved to our servers, your device's permanent gallery, or any database.

---

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

Except for the temporary, in-the-moment processing of the camera image described in Section 2, Wispora does **not collect** any of the following:

- Personally identifiable information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers  
- Usage analytics  

No user data is requested, processed, or stored permanently by the application.

---

## 4. Internet Connectivity & Backend Services

Wispora may connect to remote servers (such as cloud-based services) to deliver functionality, such as cloud-based classification or other app features. When an image is temporarily sent to our backend for prediction, the transmission is secured, the image is processed in-memory, and it is immediately deleted. These services do **not collect or log any user-specific or personal data**. All interactions are anonymous and strictly functional in nature.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

Wispora does **not integrate** or rely on third-party services such as:

- Advertising platforms (e.g., Google AdMob)  
- Analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)  
- Social login or sign-in services  

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Wispora does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. As no user data is collected, stored, or shared from anyone, the app is safe for general audiences, including minors.
